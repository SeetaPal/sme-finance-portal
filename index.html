About Us

SME Finance Facilitation Centre has been initiated as the division of SME Chamber of India to provide support and assistance to SMEs from manufacturing and service sectors to obtain bank finance, export finance & investments and PE/VC Funds for their business activity by integrating and interacting with various Banks and Financial Institutions.

The Centre will have benefits of industry experts on panel and will provide specialized services for Debt Syndication, Debt Restructuring, Buyers’ and Suppliers’ Credit, Project Finance, M&A, preparing Financial Proposals, Effective Business Plans, Credit Monitoring Arrangements, Project Reports, Market Research Reports, Formulate Risk Management Strategies, Channelise Private Equity, Venture Capital, External Commercial Borrowings and assistance for availing Factoring and Forfaiting services.

The Centre will organise various activities, Seminars, Workshops, Conferences, Interactive Sessions and lectures on Financial and Business Management to enhance the knowledge of SME Sector as well as create awareness and educate SMEs on various aspects.

Objectives

  • Empower SMEs for better interaction with Banks and Financial Institutions
  • Channelizing adequate and timely finance and investment
  • Pursuing with Banks, EXIM Bank, ECGC, SIDBI, RBI, FIs, Rating Agencies, CIBIL, Ministry of Finance and MSME and take up relevant issues
  • Provide financial management training and education
  • Establish strategic partnerships with Investors
  • Preparing SMEs for Finance & Investment
  • Restructuring & Revival of Sick SME Industries
  • Assist in creating a financial governance model for the business
  • Assist in taking up the SME sector issues with the opinion makers, government agencies and regulators

Activities

  • Integration between SMEs & Banks and FIs
  • Organise Knowledge Transmission Programs
  • Take up issues with the concerned authorities
  • Documentation and Formalities
  • Trade Promotional Activities
  • Preparation for entering into Capital Market
  • Interactive Sessions with Policy Makers and Stake Holders
  • Periodicals / Newsletter for enhancement of knowledge
  • Creating awareness for avoidance of NPA
  • Promote services of Exim Bank, ECGC, SIDBI, NSIC and Insurance bodies

INITIATED BY

SME Chamber Of India

SME Chamber of India, a premier national Chamber has been working for the development of SMEs from manufacturing, service sectors and allied industrial / business sectors for the last 32 years. The Chamber integrates SMEs, large corporates, MNCs, banks, investors, policy makers, Young & Women entrepreneurs and Start–Ups to establish and enhance contacts for better business growth and expansion.

The Chamber has been providing unique support to SMEs, Young & Women entrepreneurs and Start–Ups to convert business ideas and new concepts into profitable business ventures as well as strengthening family manage businesses for better development as well as transition and transformation.

The Chamber supports SMEs, Young & Women Entrepreneurs, and Start–Ups in converting business ideas into profitable ventures and transforming family-run businesses.
